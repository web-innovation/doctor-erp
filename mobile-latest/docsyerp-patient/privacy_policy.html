1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how DocsyERP Patient ("the App"), provided by your clinic, collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information. The App allows patients to view and request appointments, access prescriptions, and view billing information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account and Authentication: phone number, email (used for OTP delivery), user name.
  • Patient Records: patient name, age, gender (if provided), patient ID.
  • Appointments: requested dates/times, doctor ID, appointment type, symptoms, notes, status.
  • Prescriptions & Billing: prescription items, medicine names/dosage, bill amounts, payment status.
  • Device & Usage: device model, OS version, crash reports, basic analytics (if enabled).

3. How We Use Data

We use data to provide core app functionality (booking and viewing appointments, prescriptions, and bills), to send OTPs, and to improve the app. Data may be used for debugging and analytics if enabled.

4. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with the clinic and authorized clinic staff (doctors, reception, billing) as needed. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Security

Patient data is stored on the clinic's backend. Clinics should secure servers with TLS and appropriate access controls. If you are a clinic operator, ensure encryption at rest and role-based access controls.

6. User Rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting the clinic administrator.
