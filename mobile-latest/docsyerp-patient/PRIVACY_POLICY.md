1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how DocsyERP Patient ("the App"), provided by your clinic, collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information. The App allows patients to view and request appointments, access prescriptions, and view billing information.

2. Data Controller / Contact
The data controller is the clinic running the DocsyERP service. For privacy questions, contact your clinic administrator or the email provided in the Play Store listing.

3. Data We Collect
- Account and Authentication: phone number, email (used for OTP delivery), user name.
- Patient Records: patient name, age, gender (if provided), patient ID.
- Appointments: requested dates/times, doctor ID, appointment type, symptoms, notes, status.
- Prescriptions & Billing: prescription items, medicine names/dosage, bill amounts, payment status.
- Device & Usage: device model, OS version, crash reports, basic analytics for app performance (if enabled).

4. How We Use Data
- Provide core app functionality: booking appointments, showing prescriptions and bills.
- Send OTP codes and notifications (via clinic email/SMS gateways).
- Improve the app and debug issues through logs and crash reports.

5. Data Sharing
- Data is shared only with the clinic and authorized clinic staff (doctors, reception, billing) as needed.
- We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Data Storage & Security
- Patient data is stored on the clinic's backend servers. Clinics should secure servers with appropriate measures (TLS in transit, encryption at rest, access controls).
- Access is role-based: only authorized staff can view or modify patient data.

7. User Rights
- You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting your clinic.

8. Children's Data
- The app is not designed for children under 13. If the app collects data about minors, parental consent procedures must be followed by the clinic.

9. International Transfers
- Data may be stored and processed in the clinic's hosting region. Clinics operating across borders should ensure compliance with local laws.

10. Changes to this Policy
- Clinics may update this privacy notice. Users will be notified via app or clinic communication.

11. Hosting the Policy
- Host this file on your clinic website (HTTPS) and provide the URL in the Play Console Data Safety & Privacy Policy field.
