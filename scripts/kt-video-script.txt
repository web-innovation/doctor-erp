================================================================================
           DOCTOR KT VIDEO SCRIPT - DocClinic ERP System
================================================================================

INTRODUCTION (30 seconds)
-------------------------
"Welcome to the DocClinic ERP Knowledge Transfer session. This video will guide 
you through using the system for your daily practice - managing patients, 
appointments, prescriptions, and billing."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: LOGIN & DASHBOARD (1-2 minutes)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACTIONS:
1. Navigate to login page
2. Enter credentials
3. Click Login

NARRATION:
"Let's start by logging into the system. Enter your email and password, then 
click Login. You'll be taken to your Dashboard."

"The Dashboard shows you:
- Today's appointments at a glance
- Pending prescriptions that need attention
- Patient statistics 
- Quick action buttons for common tasks"

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: PATIENT MANAGEMENT (2-3 minutes)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACTIONS:
1. Click "Patients" in sidebar
2. Show search/filter options
3. Click on a patient row
4. Navigate through patient details tabs
5. Show "Add New Patient" button

NARRATION:
"Click 'Patients' in the left sidebar to access patient management."

"Here you can see all your patients. Use the search bar to find patients by 
name, phone, or ID. You can also filter by various criteria."

"Click on any patient to view their complete profile including:
- Personal and contact information
- Complete medical history
- Previous visit records
- All prescriptions issued"

"To add a new patient, click the 'Add Patient' button and fill in the 
required information."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: APPOINTMENTS (2-3 minutes)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACTIONS:
1. Click "Appointments" in sidebar
2. Show calendar view
3. Click on an appointment
4. Create new appointment
5. Change appointment status

NARRATION:
"The Appointments section shows your schedule in a calendar view."

"You can see all appointments for the day, week, or month. Click on any 
appointment to view details or make changes."

"To create a new appointment:
1. Click 'New Appointment'
2. Select the patient from the dropdown
3. Choose the date and time
4. Add any notes for the visit
5. Click Save"

"You can update appointment status to Pending, Confirmed, Completed, or 
Cancelled as needed."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: PRESCRIPTIONS (2-3 minutes)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACTIONS:
1. Click "Prescriptions" in sidebar
2. Show prescription list
3. Click "New Prescription"
4. Fill prescription form
5. Show print/download options

NARRATION:
"The Prescriptions section is where you create and manage patient prescriptions."

"To create a new prescription:
1. Click 'New Prescription'
2. Select the patient
3. Add the diagnosis
4. Add medications - enter the drug name, dosage, frequency, and duration
5. You can add multiple medications to one prescription
6. Add any special instructions
7. Click Save"

"Once saved, you can:
- Print the prescription for the patient
- Download as PDF
- View it anytime in the patient's history"

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: BILLING (1-2 minutes)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACTIONS:
1. Click "Billing" in sidebar
2. Show billing list
3. Create new bill
4. Add line items
5. Generate invoice

NARRATION:
"The Billing section handles all financial transactions."

"To create a bill:
1. Click 'New Bill'
2. Select the patient
3. Add services - consultation, procedures, tests
4. The system calculates totals automatically
5. Generate and print the invoice"

"You can view billing history and track payments here as well."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: REPORTS & SETTINGS (1 minute)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACTIONS:
1. Quick tour of Reports
2. Quick tour of Settings

NARRATION:
"The Reports section provides insights into your practice - patient visits, 
revenue, and other metrics."

"In Settings, you can update your profile, change password, and customize 
your preferences."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: WHATSAPP INTEGRATION (30 seconds)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
NARRATION:
"DocClinic also integrates with WhatsApp. Patients can:
- Book appointments via WhatsApp
- Receive reminders
- Get prescription notifications"

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONCLUSION (30 seconds)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
NARRATION:
"That covers the main features of DocClinic ERP for doctors. The system is 
designed to streamline your workflow and give you more time with patients."

"If you have questions, contact the IT support team."

"Thank you for watching!"

================================================================================
                    TOTAL VIDEO TIME: 10-15 minutes
================================================================================

RECORDING TIPS:
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Pause 2-3 seconds between sections
- Move mouse smoothly to guide viewer's attention
- Keep browser window maximized
- Close unnecessary tabs/notifications before recording
